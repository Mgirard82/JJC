The Yurt Experience

What is a yurt?

A yurt is a round, portable tent used by nomads in Central Asia. Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

Sleeping accommodations

Each yurt sleeps two comfortably with a king-sized bed. Linens and towels are provided.

Bathroom facilities

Each yurt has a private bathroom with shower.

Kitchen amenities

Each yurt has a microwave, mini-fridge, and a sink.

Entertainment

Each yurt is equipped with a flat-screen TV, satellite TV, and a DVD player.

Climate control

Each yurt has a gas fireplace and a ceiling fan.
